Who was Harper Lee?

Harper Lee was an American novelist. She born on April 28, 1926. She grew up in Monroeville, a small town in southwest Alabama. As a child, Lee was a tomboy and a precocious reader. After she graduated from Alabama University, she wrote several essays and short stories. Harper Lee is best known for her Pulitzer Prize-winning novel To Kill a Mockingbird which was published in 1960.Also, she wrote a second novel, Go Set a Watchman. She died at the age of 89 on February 19, 2016.
